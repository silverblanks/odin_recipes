Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a yummy treat

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Ground Beef
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Tomatoe Sauce

Steps:

  1. Buy some lasagna pasta
  2. Spoon sauce into the bottom of a dish
  3. Add 4 Lasagna Noodles
  4. Spread ricotta cheeese on top
  5. Repeat the layer two more times
  6. Cover and bake at 375F for 30 minutes
