Omelette

Description

An omelette is a yummy treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wisk the eggs until the yolks and whites are uniform, salt to taste
  2. Head up a pan and place a bit of butter in, wait until it sizzles
  3. Place your egg mix into the pan and constantly stir, do not let large curds form
  4. Once the mixture is mostly solid, turn the heat to low and spread the mix evenly
  5. Use your spatula to wrap the egg into a neat roll
  6. Enjoy!
